Minecraft Prom 2020

May 23, 7-10pm PST

Unfortunately we are unable to host a physical prom this year so we will be hosting a Minecraft prom instead!



This year's prom tickets would have cost $50 per person. Whilst our event is completely free to attend we encourage everyone to donate $50 to Feeding America.


How to join

1. Get a Minecraft account ($27)
2. Install Minecraft Java Edition build 1.15.2 (PC/Mac) | Instructions
3. Add this address to your server list: server.mcprom.net | Instructions

How to listen along

Press "T" and then follow the link in-game or type "/audio" to launch OpenAudioMC which will play music through your browser.
